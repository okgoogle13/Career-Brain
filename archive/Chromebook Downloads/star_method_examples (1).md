# STAR Method Resume Bullet Points - Community Services

## Example 1: Crisis Intervention Specialist
• Responded to 150+ after-hours crisis calls involving clients with acute mental health episodes and suicide risk
• Utilised trauma-informed communication and de-escalation techniques to ensure safe crisis resolution in 100% of cases
• Coordinated emergency interventions with police, ambulance, and hospital services across metropolitan region
• Reduced repeat crisis presentations by 40% through development of personalised safety plans and follow-up protocols

## Example 2: Program Coordinator - Youth Employment
• Designed employment readiness program targeting 25 young people exiting out-of-home care annually
• Conducted comprehensive needs assessments and established partnerships with 8 TAFE institutes and 15 local employers
• Delivered 8-week program covering resume writing, interview skills, and workplace expectations to 40+ participants
• Achieved 75% program completion rate with 60% of graduates securing employment within 3 months

## Example 3: Intake and Assessment Officer - Homelessness Services
• Conducted comprehensive intake assessments for 200+ individuals and families experiencing homelessness annually
• Utilised validated assessment tools including VI-SPDAT and NDIS eligibility criteria to determine service needs and priority levels
• Collaborated with housing providers, mental health services, and Centrelink to verify client information and coordinate referrals
• Maintained 95% accuracy rate in eligibility determinations while reducing average assessment time from 90 to 45 minutes

## Example 4: AOD Support Worker - Community Treatment Program
• Provided individual and group counselling support to 45+ clients with alcohol and drug dependencies using motivational interviewing techniques
• Delivered structured relapse prevention programs including cognitive behavioural therapy and mindfulness-based interventions
• Coordinated with GPs, psychiatrists, and pharmacotherapy providers to support clients with dual diagnosis presentations
• Achieved 70% client retention rate over 12-month treatment episodes and 65% successful completion of treatment goals

## Example 5: Disability Support Coordinator
• Implemented positive behaviour support plans for 20+ clients with intellectual disabilities in residential settings
• Collaborated with behaviour specialists to conduct functional assessments and identify environmental triggers
• Delivered intensive staff training on de-escalation techniques and alternative communication strategies to 25 support workers
• Reduced challenging behaviours by 70% within 3 months while maintaining client dignity and community participation

## Example 6: Client Advocate - Elder Abuse Prevention
• Investigated suspected financial elder abuse cases involving unauthorized withdrawals exceeding $15,000
• Coordinated multi-agency responses with Public Guardian, police, and banking institutions to protect client assets
• Supported clients through legal processes while facilitating family mediation to preserve relationships
• Successfully recovered $12,000+ in stolen funds and established ongoing financial safeguards for vulnerable clients

## Example 7: Group Facilitator - Domestic Violence Support
• Identified barriers to program participation including lack of childcare and transport for 65% of eligible women
• Established on-site childcare with qualified educators and negotiated transport vouchers with local council
• Redesigned group format with flexible attendance options and relocated to accessible community centre
• Increased retention rates from 35% to 80% and expanded program from 1 to 3 weekly support groups

## Example 8: Team Leader - Residential Services
• Led team of 15 support workers across residential disability services experiencing 45% annual staff turnover
• Conducted exit interviews with departing staff to identify systemic issues affecting retention and service quality
• Implemented peer mentoring program, flexible rostering system, and regular clinical supervision framework
• Reduced staff turnover to 18% within 12 months while achieving 100% compliance in quality audits

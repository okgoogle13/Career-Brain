## Artifact 4: Organization Research Template

### Pre-Application Research Framework

#### Organizational Profile
- **Legal Structure:** Government department, NGO, for-profit, social enterprise
- **Funding Sources:** Government contracts, donations, fee-for-service, grants
- **Service Areas:** Geographic coverage and population groups served
- **Organizational Size:** Staff numbers, budget, service volume
- **Governance Structure:** Board composition, advisory committees, stakeholder involvement

#### Mission and Values Analysis
- **Mission Statement:** [Insert organization's exact mission statement]
- **Core Values:** [List and briefly explain each organizational value]
- **Strategic Priorities:** [Current strategic plan objectives and timelines]
- **Recent Initiatives:** [New programs, policy changes, organizational developments]

#### Sector Context and Positioning
- **Key Competitors/Partners:** Other organizations in similar space
- **Sector Reputation:** Media coverage, sector recognition, professional standing
- **Innovation Profile:** New approaches, technology adoption, practice leadership
- **Challenges Faced:** Current organizational or sector challenges they're addressing

#### Cultural Indicators
- **Communication Style:** Formal/informal tone in public communications
- **Diversity and Inclusion:** Stated commitments and visible practices
- **Professional Development:** Investment in staff training and career progression
- **Work Environment:** Office culture, flexibility, professional atmosphere

### Research Sources Checklist
- [ ] Official website and annual reports
- [ ] Recent media coverage and press releases  
- [ ] Social media presence and engagement style
- [ ] Industry publications and sector reports
- [ ] Professional networks and personal connections
- [ ] Government databases and funding announcements
- [ ] Job advertisement language and requirements emphasis

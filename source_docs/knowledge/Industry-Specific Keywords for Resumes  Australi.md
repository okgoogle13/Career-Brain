# Industry-Specific Keywords for Resumes (Australian Community Services & Social Work)

---

## Core Community Services & Social Work Terminology
* **Case Management:** Assessment, planning, coordination, monitoring, review, advocacy.
* **Client-Centred Practice:** Individualised support, person-centred approach, client voice.
* **Trauma-Informed Care:** Understanding and responding to the impact of trauma.
* **Strengths-Based Approach:** Focusing on client strengths and resilience.
* **Cultural Responsiveness/Competence:** Working effectively with diverse cultural backgrounds, including Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities.
* **Crisis Intervention:** Immediate support in crisis situations, de-escalation, safety planning.
* **Advocacy:** Individual advocacy, systemic advocacy, rights-based practice.
* **Psychosocial Assessment:** Comprehensive assessment of an individual's psychological and social factors.
* **Group Facilitation:** Leading therapeutic or support groups.
* **Community Development:** Empowerment, capacity building, community engagement.
* **Ethical Practice:** Adherence to professional codes of ethics (e.g., AASW Code of Ethics, ACWA Code of Ethics).
* **Confidentiality & Privacy:** Upholding client privacy and data protection.
* **Mandatory Reporting:** Adherence to child protection and elder abuse reporting requirements.
* **Risk Assessment & Management:** Identifying and mitigating risks to clients or staff.

## Australian Sector-Specific Terms & Acronyms
* **NDIS (National Disability Insurance Scheme):** Support Coordination, Plan Management, Self-Management, Capacity Building, Core Supports, Specialist Disability Accommodation (SDA), Quality and Safeguarding Framework.
* **Child Protection:** Child Safe Standards, Working With Children Check (WWCC), Guardianship orders, Out-of-Home Care (OOHC), Child Safety Services.
* **Aged Care:** Aged Care Quality Standards, Home Care Packages (HCP), Residential Aged Care, My Aged Care.
* **Mental Health:** Clinical mental health, community mental health, mental health first aid, psychosocial recovery coaching.
* **AOD (Alcohol and Other Drugs):** Harm minimisation, substance abuse counselling, relapse prevention.
* **Domestic and Family Violence (DFV):** Safety planning, risk assessment, support services, perpetrator intervention.
* **Homelessness Services:** Crisis accommodation, transitional housing, tenancy support, outreach.
* **Youth Justice:** Diversion programs, youth offending, restorative justice.
* **Family Support:** Early intervention, family preservation, parenting programs.
* **Not-for-Profit (NFP)/Non-Government Organisation (NGO):** Common organisational structures.
* **DFFH (Department of Families, Fairness and Housing - VIC equivalent):** State-specific government departments.
* **DCJ (Department of Communities and Justice - NSW equivalent):** State-specific government departments.
* **DHHS (Department of Health and Human Services - former name, still sometimes seen).**
* **Royal Commissions:** (e.g., Royal Commission into Institutional Responses to Child Sexual Abuse, Royal Commission into Aged Care Quality and Safety) - demonstrating awareness.

## Relevant Australian Legislation & Frameworks (mentioning awareness/application of)
* **National Disability Insurance Scheme Act 2013**
* **Child Protection legislation (state-specific, e.g., Children, Youth and Families Act 2005 VIC)**
* **Aged Care Act 1997**
* **Family Law Act 1975**
* **Social Security Act 1991**
* **Work Health and Safety (WHS) Act (Model WHS Laws)**
* **Privacy Act 1988**
* **State-specific Mental Health Acts.**
* **Ethical Frameworks:** AASW Code of Ethics, ACWA Code of Ethics and Practice Guidelines.

## Software & Tools (Common in Australian Community Services)
* **Client Management Systems (CMS):** Salesforce, Penelope, CareMaster, SHIP, Procura, ECIS.
* **Microsoft Office Suite:** Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint.
* **Reporting Tools:** Power BI, Tableau (for data analysis in larger organisations).
* **Telehealth Platforms:** Zoom, Microsoft Teams.
* **Data Entry & Record Keeping Systems.**

## Professional Bodies & Registrations (Key for Social Workers/Community Workers)
* **AASW (Australian Association of Social Workers):** Required for many social worker roles, Accredited Mental Health Social Worker (AMHSW).
* **ACWA (Australian Community Workers Association):** Professional body for community workers.
* **Working With Children Check (WWCC)/Blue Card/Ochre Card:** State-specific requirements.
* **NDIS Worker Screening Check.**
* **First Aid & CPR Certification.**

## Artifact 5: Competency Mapping Framework

### Core Community Services Competencies

#### Technical Competencies
**Assessment and Planning**
- Risk assessment and safety planning
- Comprehensive needs assessment
- Care planning and goal setting
- Progress monitoring and evaluation
- Case documentation and record keeping

**Intervention and Support**
- Crisis intervention and de-escalation
- Counseling and therapeutic communication
- Advocacy and empowerment approaches
- Referral and service coordination
- Group facilitation and program delivery

**Professional Practice**
- Ethical decision-making and professional boundaries
- Cultural competency and anti-oppressive practice
- Evidence-based practice integration
- Reflective practice and continuous improvement
- Legal and regulatory compliance

#### Leadership and Management Competencies
**People Management**
- Staff supervision and performance management
- Team development and motivation
- Recruitment and selection processes
- Professional development planning
- Conflict resolution and mediation

**Operational Management** 
- Program planning and implementation
- Budget management and resource allocation
- Quality assurance and continuous improvement
- Risk management and compliance oversight
- Stakeholder engagement and partnership development

**Strategic Leadership**
- Strategic planning and organizational development
- Change management and innovation leadership
- Policy development and advocacy
- Community engagement and consultation
- Sector leadership and professional representation

### Competency Weighting by Role Level

#### Frontline Positions (Higher weighting)
- Direct client support and intervention skills
- Communication and interpersonal abilities
- Cultural competency and inclusive practice
- Professional ethics and boundary management
- Team collaboration and support

#### Supervisory Positions (Balanced weighting)
- Clinical/professional supervision capabilities
- Performance management and staff development
- Program coordination and service delivery
- Quality assurance and compliance management
- Stakeholder communication and liaison

#### Management Positions (Higher weighting)
- Strategic planning and organizational leadership
- Financial management and resource optimization
- Policy development and sector engagement
- Change management and innovation leadership
- Governance and accountability frameworks

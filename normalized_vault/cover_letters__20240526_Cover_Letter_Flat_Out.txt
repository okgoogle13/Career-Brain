colonization, white supremacy, transphobia, and transmisogyny in driving criminalization and incarceration. 
My lived experience, combined with my volunteer work at Thorne Harbour Health, has provided me with a 
nuanced understanding of the challenges faced by the LGBTQIA+ community.  
Throughout my career, I have consistently demonstrated the capacity to build trust and rapport with 
individuals experiencing intersecting oppressions rapidly. My empathetic and non-judgmental approach, 
honed through extensive experience working alongside survivors of trauma and systems abuse, enables 
me to provide compassionate support while maintaining clear, respectful boundaries and the importance of 
person-centred, compassionate support responses in times of crisis.  
I possess a strong knowledge of legal systems, housing services, and other support services available in 
the community for trans and gender-diverse people in Naarm. My advocacy skills, refined through 
navigating complex systems during my time in the corporate world, empower me to be a strategic and 
effective advocate for those in need.  
I am adept at balancing competing priorities, managing time effectively, and working independently and 
collaboratively within a team. My high-level written, verbal, and interpersonal skills enable me to 
communicate effectively with diverse stakeholders, including managers, staff, supported individuals, 
support services, government agencies, and community groups.  
My passion for social justice, diverse experience and skillset make me a strong candidate for this position.

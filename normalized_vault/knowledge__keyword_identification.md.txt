Scrutinize the entire job description, focusing on the "Requirements," "Responsibilities," and "Skills" sections. 
Identify Skills & Qualifications:
Look for specific skills, software proficiency, languages, or other qualifications mentioned as requirements or preferred. 
Note Repeated Terms:
Pay attention to words and phrases that appear multiple times, as these are often the most important keywords for the role. 
Consider Action Verbs:
Look for action verbs used to describe job duties, as these can indicate the skills and responsibilities the employer is looking for. 
Use a Keyword Finder:
Some tools can analyze job descriptions and identify relevant keywords.

# Resume Action Verbs & Power Words (Australian Context: Social Work & Community Services)

Use these strong verbs at the beginning of your bullet points to describe achievements and responsibilities, reflecting the unique contributions in the Australian Community Services and Social Work sectors.

---

## Direct Client Support & Casework
* **Assessed:** Conducted comprehensive needs assessments for clients.
* **Counseled:** Provided individual or group counselling sessions.
* **Supported:** Offered empathetic and practical support to vulnerable individuals/families.
* **Case-managed:** Coordinated and managed complex client caseloads.
* **Referred:** Connected clients to relevant external services and resources.
* **Intervened:** Provided crisis intervention and de-escalation support.
* **Facilitated:** Led group programs, workshops, or client meetings.
* **Empowered:** Enabled clients to make informed decisions and build self-efficacy.
* **Advocated:** Represented clients' best interests to secure rights and access services.
* **Liaised:** Maintained effective communication with clients, families, and stakeholders.
* **Educated:** Provided psycho-education and skill-building sessions to clients.
* **Reviewed:** Monitored client progress and reviewed care plans regularly.
* **Developed:** Created individualised support plans (ISPs) or care plans.
* **Protected:** Ensured the safety and wellbeing of children and vulnerable adults.

## Collaboration & Interdisciplinary Practice
* **Collaborated:** Worked effectively within multidisciplinary and interagency teams.
* **Coordinated:** Managed complex service delivery across multiple providers.
* **Partnered:** Built strong relationships with community organisations and stakeholders.
* **Consulted:** Provided expert advice to colleagues, families, or external agencies.
* **Engaged:** Fostered positive relationships with diverse community groups.
* **Negotiated:** Achieved mutually beneficial outcomes in complex client situations.
* **Disseminated:** Shared information and resources effectively across teams.

## Program Development & Management
* **Designed:** Developed new programs or initiatives to meet community needs.
* **Implemented:** Successfully rolled out new services or program components.
* **Evaluated:** Assessed program effectiveness and recommended improvements.
* **Managed:** Oversaw program operations, including budgeting and resource allocation.
* **Secured:** Successfully applied for grants or funding for programs.
* **Oversaw:** Supervised staff, volunteers, or student placements.
* **Streamlined:** Improved efficiency of administrative or service delivery processes.

## Research, Reporting & Compliance
* **Analyzed:** Interpreted complex client data or social research findings.
* **Documented:** Maintained accurate and timely client records and reports.
* **Reported:** Prepared statutory reports or funding submissions.
* **Researched:** Investigated best practices and evidence-based interventions.
* **Monitored:** Tracked client progress and program outcomes.
* **Complied:** Ensured adherence to legislative requirements and ethical guidelines (e.g., AASW Code of Ethics, NDIS Quality and Safeguarding Framework).
* **Audited:** Reviewed cases or processes for compliance and quality assurance.

## General Professional Skills
* **Adapted:** Adjusted approaches to meet diverse client needs and changing contexts.
* **Problem-solved:** Addressed complex challenges with innovative and practical solutions.
* **Trained:** Delivered training and professional development to staff or community members.
* **Mentored:** Guided and supported junior staff or students.
* **Communicated:** Articulated complex information clearly and empathetically.
* **Organised:** Managed multiple priorities and maintained efficient workflows.

## Artifact 2: Australian Community Services Sector Reference

### Legislative and Regulatory Framework

#### Commonwealth Legislation
- **Disability Discrimination Act 1992:** Anti-discrimination protections for people with disabilities
- **Human Rights Commission Act 1986:** Framework for human rights protection and complaint processes
- **Privacy Act 1988:** Privacy principles and information management requirements
- **Work Health and Safety Act 2011:** Workplace safety obligations and risk management
- **Fair Work Act 2009:** Employment relations and workplace rights framework
- **National Disability Insurance Scheme Act 2013:** NDIS framework and participant rights

#### State-Specific Legislation (Adapt for your state)
- Child Protection Acts and mandatory reporting requirements
- Mental Health Acts and involuntary treatment provisions
- Community Services Acts and service delivery standards
- Guardianship and Administration Acts for decision-making support
- Carers Recognition Acts and support obligations

### Professional Standards and Frameworks

#### National Practice Standards
- **National Standards for Disability Services:** Quality and safeguarding requirements
- **National Safety and Quality Health Service Standards:** Healthcare service delivery standards
- **Australian Social Work Practice Standards:** Professional practice requirements for social workers
- **Community Services and Health Industry Skills Council Standards:** Competency frameworks

#### Evidence-Based Practice Models
- **Trauma-Informed Care:** Understanding trauma impacts and responsive service design
- **Strengths-Based Practice:** Focus on client capabilities and empowerment approaches
- **Person-Centred Practice:** Individual choice and control in service delivery
- **Cultural Responsiveness:** Culturally appropriate service delivery methods
- **Recovery-Oriented Practice:** Supporting individual recovery journeys and goals

### Current Sector Terminology

#### Service Delivery Models
- **Wraparound Services:** Comprehensive, coordinated support across multiple life domains
- **Assertive Outreach:** Proactive engagement with hard-to-reach populations
- **Stepped Care:** Graduated intervention intensity based on need and response
- **Integrated Service Delivery:** Coordinated multi-agency service provision
- **Place-Based Approaches:** Community-focused service design and delivery

#### Client-Focused Language
- **Person-First Language:** "Person with disability" rather than "disabled person"
- **Consumer/Participant:** Preferred term for service users in many contexts
- **Co-design:** Collaborative service development with service users
- **Self-Advocacy:** Supporting individuals to represent their own interests
- **Supported Decision-Making:** Assistance with decision-making while maintaining autonomy

#### Professional Practice Terms
- **Reflective Practice:** Systematic reflection on professional actions and outcomes
- **Clinical Supervision:** Professional oversight and development processes
- **Professional Boundaries:** Ethical limits in professional relationships
- **Duty of Care:** Legal and ethical obligation to prevent harm
- **Mandatory Reporting:** Legal requirement to report suspected abuse or neglect

### Contemporary Sector Challenges

#### Policy and Funding Issues
- NDIS implementation challenges and market development
- Aged care reform and quality improvement initiatives
- Housing affordability and homelessness service responses
- Mental health system reform and community integration
- Child protection system improvements and family preservation

#### Service Delivery Challenges
- Workforce shortages and retention difficulties
- Rural and remote service access inequities
- Technology integration and digital inclusion
- COVID-19 ongoing impacts on service delivery
- Complex needs populations requiring specialized responses
